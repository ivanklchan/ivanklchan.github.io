Greetings !

Welcome to my website! You may have came to this website from my social media, or maybe I told you about it. Regardless, I hope you find my personal website informative. Let's start with a brief summary of myself:

I am a 3rd Year Medical Radiation Sciences student at McMaster University specializing in Radiography. Radiography uses X-rays to produce images of the human body. Basically, I am studying to become an X-ray Technologist, I will be the Health Care Professional that perform X-ray procedures on you when you visit the hospital.

I attended my first Clinical Practicum at Hamilton General Hospital. I am competent in performing regular X-ray procedures and I am committed to give excellent patient care.

I learned HTML/CSS online in the summer of 2019 and began coding right away. I have a passion to become a Web Developer along side with my primary career in the future! This website is my first personal project, I will be updating my blogs, research projects, and my insights in life regularly :D
